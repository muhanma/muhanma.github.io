    About Divvy

    Divvy is Chicagoland’s bike share system. Divvy provides residents and visitors with a convenient, and environmental transportation option for getting from Point A to Point B.

    A problem: no space to park and no available bike

    Unbalanced distribution of Divvy bikes in different stations is a big issue that harms the usability and discounts the user experience of the Divvy service. Users are always struggling with no space to dock at hot spots, or during rush hour, and no available bikes at docking stations in remote regions.

    Our Solution

    Divvy Plus is a mobile app that is not only a bike finder but also a plan maker.​ it helps users get real-time information of docking stations and find bikes that are available nearby more efficiently, Plans recommended routes based on the real-time distribution of bikes.​and assigns a mission that rewards users to move the bikes from a busy station to an empty one.
