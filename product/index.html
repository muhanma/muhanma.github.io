101 Design Method Cards

A Structed Approach for Driving Innovation

Know Context

We study the circumstances or events that affect the environment in which our innovation offerings exist or could exsit.

Know People

In this mode our goal is to understand people and their interactions with everything during their daily lives.

Frame Insights

After conducting research, the next challenge is to bring structure to what has been found and learned from the previous modes.

Explore Concepts

In this mode we do structured brainstorming to identify opportunites and to explore new concepts.

Frame Solutions

In this mode, we build on the large set of concepts that have been developed earlier by combining them to form system of concepts.

Realize Offerings

Once potential solutions are framed and prototypes tested,they need to be evaluated to move to implementation.
